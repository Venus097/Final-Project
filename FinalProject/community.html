The Sicilian Defense

A popular and aggressive response to 1.e4, where Black plays 1...c5 to control the center from the flank. It leads to dynamic and sharp positions, often favoring counterattacks.

The Fork Tactic

A tactical maneuver where one piece (usually a knight or pawn) attacks two or more enemy pieces at once. This forces the opponent into a disadvantageous position.

Zugzwang

A strategic concept where any move the opponent makes weakens their position. This technique is often used in endgames to force the opponent into making losing moves.
